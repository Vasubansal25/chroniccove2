Chronic diseases are long-lasting conditions that typically develop slowly and persist over an extended period, often for years or even a lifetime. These diseases may not have a cure, but they can usually be managed with ongoing treatment and lifestyle changes. Chronic diseases often result from a combination of genetic, environmental, and lifestyle factors. Some common examples of chronic diseases include diabetes, heart disease, hypertension, arthritis, asthma, chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD), cancer, and autoimmune disorders like rheumatoid arthritis and lupus.

Our Service

Remote Monitoring:

We provide specialized monitoring devices equipped with sensors to track vital signs such as heart rate, respiratory rate, temperature, and activity levels in pets with chronic diseases..

Remote Consultations:

We facilitate remote consultations with veterinary professionals through video conferencing or chat features integrated into the mobile app, enabling pet owners to discuss their pet's health status and receive expert advice remotely.

24 Hour Support

If you have a health concern and can’t reach a doctor right away, or you’re not sure where else to call, you can call our Medical Help Line 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. Once enrolled, this member benefit allows you to speak with an experienced registered nurse to get answers to your questions and find out what steps you need to take. If you are currently a member of Independent Health, you can find the phone number of the 24-Hour Medical Help Line on the back of your Member ID card.

Continuous Support and Education:

We offer ongoing support and educational resources to pet owners, including articles, videos, and webinars covering topics related to pet care, chronic disease management, and effective use of the monitoring system, empowering them to become proactive advocates for their pet's health..

Quick Response

Our solution integrates quick response functionalities into the webside, allowing quick access to veterinary assistance or emergency services in case of critical health events, ensuring prompt and effective care for pets in need.



Medication Reminders:

Our app includes medication reminder features to help pet owners adhere to prescribed treatment plans, with customizable schedules and dosage instructions to ensure optimal care for their pets..

Physical Therapy:

"Physical therapy is a specialized form of rehabilitation that focuses on restoring movement and function. Through targeted exercises, manual therapy, and other techniques, physical therapists help patients recover from injuries, surgeries, or chronic conditions. Sessions are tailored to each individual's needs and goals, promoting mobility, strength, and flexibility. Physical therapy can alleviate pain, improve balance, and prevent further injury. It is a holistic approach that considers the body as a whole, addressing underlying issues to enhance overall well-being. Patients receive personalized attention and guidance to optimize their recovery and achieve long-term success. With the guidance of a skilled physical therapist, individuals regain independence and regain confidence in their abilities. From athletes recovering from sports injuries to seniors improving their mobility, physical therapy offers valuable support for people of all ages and backgrounds."
